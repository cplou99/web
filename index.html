About me

I am a Ph.D. student specializing in Computer Vision and Deep Learning at the RoPeRT research group, University of Zaragoza, Spain. My academic journey began with a B.Sc. in Mathematics, followed by an M.Sc. in Robotics, Graphics, and Computer Vision, both of which I completed with honors.

Ph.D. Student in Computer Vision and Deep Learning

I am finishing the second year of my Ph.D. thesis, focusing on various tasks in video understanding, such as action recognition, temporal grounding, and video question answering in challenging scenarios like extremely long-duration videos, event-based data, and egocentric perspectives. I am also interested in applying Bayesian deep learning to computer vision tasks and transferring state-of-the-art computer vision and deep learning models to robotics.

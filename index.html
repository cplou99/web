About me

I'm a PhD student specializing in Computer Vision and Deep Learning at the RoPeRT research group, University of Zaragoza, Spain. To reach this point, I firstly studied Mathematics and, in search of an area where I could apply the acquired knowledge, I decided to pursue a Master's in Robotics, Graphics, and Computer Vision.

I am currently working on my PhD thesis, focusing on action recognition in videos with various features, such as event-based or egocentric perspective. Additionally, I am interested in applying Bayesian deep learning to computer vision tasks and generative models.
